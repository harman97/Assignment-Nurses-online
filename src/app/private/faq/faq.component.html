What is Angular?
Angular is a TypeScript-based open-source front-end web application platform led by the Angular Team at Google and by a community of individuals and corporations. Angular is a complete rewrite from the same team that built AngularJS. But let me tell you that Angular is completely different from AngularJS.
What Are the Benefits of Angular?
Angular uses modern web platforms to deliver app-like experiences.
What Are the Features of Angular 4?
Angular 4 supports the most recent versions of TypeScript, i.e. 2.1 and 2.2. This has enhanced the rate of ngc and enabled superior type checking.
